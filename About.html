We used many technologies in the development of this website.

A few Javascripts are used in the validation and entry of the Login and Registration forms.
Bootstrap and CSS were used in order to make the website responsive and accessible on many devices.
PHP scripts are utilized in order to communicate with the database.
MYSQL was used to store Login information, emails, and each individual user's Carbon footprint.


Users are able to register an account, and use their login credentials to store and access their carbon footprint.
In the future, we hope to implement sessions, a system where you may compare your carbon footprint to others, as well as a system in which you may track trees you have planted, and how those trees help to sequester your carbon emissions.
